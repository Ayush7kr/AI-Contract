# User Walkthrough 🚶‍♂️

Welcome to LexiSure AI! This guide will take you through the typical workflow of using the platform.

---

## 1. Setting Up Your Environment
Before you start, ensure you have a **Google Gemini API Key**.
1.  Duplicate `.env.example` to `.env` in the `backend/` directory.
2.  Paste your API Key: `GEMINI_API_KEY=your_key_here`.

## 2. Registration & Onboarding
1.  Launch the app and navigate to the **Signup** page.
2.  Create your account.
3.  Upon logging in, you'll be greeted by the **Dashboard**, which shows your contract activity summary.

## 3. Uploading a Contract
1.  Go to the **Upload** page.
2.  Drag and drop your contract file (PDF or DOCX).
3.  Click **Process**. The backend will extract the text and trigger the AI analysis.

## 4. Reviewing Analysis
1.  Navigate to the **Contracts** list and select your uploaded document.
2.  On the **Analysis** page, check the overall **Risk Score**.
3.  Scroll through the **Clauses** tab to see highlighted risks and summaries provided by the AI.

## 5. Using the Legal Assistant
1.  Open the **Legal Chat** tab from the sidebar.
2.  Ask specific questions about your contract, e.g., *"Is there a non-compete clause?"*.
3.  The AI will respond based on the specific content of your document.

## 6. Negotiation & Comparison
1.  Use the **Negotiate** feature to see suggested improvements.
2.  If you have multiple versions, use **Compare Contracts** to see exactly what changed and how it affects your risk profile.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips
-   **Clear Scans**: Ensure your PDFs are text-readable (not just images) for the best extraction results.
-   **Stay Compliant**: Regularly run the **Compliance** check to see if new regulatory requirements affect your existing contracts.
