# Features Guide 🚀

LexiSure AI provides a suite of tools to empower businesses in their legal workflows. Below is a detailed look at the core functionalities.

---

## 🧐 Contract Analysis & Risk Scoring
The heart of the platform. Upload any contract and receive:
-   **Risk Score**: A 0-100 score indicating the overall risk level.
-   **Critical Clauses**: Automatic detection of Indemnification, Limitation of Liability, Termination, and more.
-   **Risk Breakdown**: Categorization of risks (Financial, Legal, Operational).

## 🤝 AI-Powered Negotiation
Stuck on a clause? The Negotiation Assistant provides:
-   **Modification Suggestions**: Alternative phrasing that better protects your interests.
-   **Impact Analysis**: Explanation of why a change is recommended and what the original clause meant for your business.

## ⚖️ Compliance Tracking
Ensure your contracts meet industry standards and local regulations:
-   **Regulatory Check**: Scans for compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, or custom company policies.
-   **Missing Clauses**: Highlights essential clauses that are absent from the document.

## 🕵️ Vendor Intelligence
Manage your vendor ecosystem with clarity:
-   **Vendor Risk Profile**: Aggregated risk data across all contracts with a specific vendor.
-   **Key Milestones**: Track renewal dates, notice periods, and payment schedules.

## 💬 Legal Assistant Chat
A dedicated interface for real-time interaction:
-   **Ask Anything**: "Does this contract allow for price increases?" or "What is the notice period for termination?"
-   **Context-Aware**: The assistant knows the details of the currently selected contract.

## 📊 Dashboard & Visualization
-   **Analytics**: View trends in contract risks over time.
-   **Activity Feed**: Keep track of recent uploads and analysis updates.
-   **Global Search**: Quickly find any contract by name, vendor, or content.

---
