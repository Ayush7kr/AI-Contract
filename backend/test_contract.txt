PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, by and between:

Party A: TechCorp Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Client")
Party B: DataFlow Analytics LLC, a California limited liability company ("Contractor")

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Contractor to provide certain professional services, and Contractor desires to perform such services, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF SERVICES
1.1 Contractor shall provide data analytics, machine learning model development, and consulting services as described in Exhibit A attached hereto.
1.2 All deliverables shall be completed within the timeline specified in the project plan.

ARTICLE 2: COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT
2.1 Client shall pay Contractor a total fee of $250,000 for the services described herein.
2.2 Payment shall be made in monthly installments of $41,666.67, due within 30 days of receipt of invoice.
2.3 Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.
2.4 Penalties for early termination shall be $75,000 plus any outstanding invoices.

ARTICLE 3: TERM AND TERMINATION
3.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of six (6) months.
3.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
3.3 This Agreement shall auto-renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides 60 days written notice of non-renewal.
3.4 Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered through the date of termination.

ARTICLE 4: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 All work product created by Contractor shall be considered "work made for hire" and shall be the sole property of Client.
4.2 Client grants Contractor a perpetual license to use general methodologies and techniques developed during the engagement.
4.3 Contractor retains all rights to pre-existing intellectual property.

ARTICLE 5: CONFIDENTIALITY
5.1 Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information disclosed by the other party.
5.2 Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement in perpetuity.
5.3 Neither party shall disclose confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.

ARTICLE 6: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
6.1 Contractor shall have unlimited liability for damages arising from breach of this Agreement.
6.2 Client shall indemnify and hold harmless Contractor against any and all claims arising from Client's use of the deliverables.
6.3 Contractor shall indemnify Client against third party intellectual property infringement claims.
6.4 IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES.

ARTICLE 7: WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS
7.1 Contractor warrants that all services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.
7.2 Contractor represents that it has the expertise and resources necessary to perform the services.
7.3 THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS-IS" WITH NO WARRANTY OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

ARTICLE 8: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
8.1 Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
8.2 The arbitration shall take place in San Francisco, California.
8.3 The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

ARTICLE 9: NON-COMPETE
9.1 During the term of this Agreement and for a period of two (2) years following termination, Contractor agrees not to provide similar services to Client's direct competitors.

ARTICLE 10: GOVERNING LAW
10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
10.2 The parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of California.

ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS
11.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.
11.2 Any modification to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.
11.3 If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
11.4 Waiver of any breach shall not constitute waiver of any subsequent breach.
11.5 The rights and obligations under this Agreement may be assigned only with prior written consent.
